Creating Memories, One Photo at a Time

Welcome to UniSelfie! I'm passionate about capturing the joy and excitement of your special moments. Our open air photobooth service brings a modern, elegant touch to any event, allowing your guests to create lasting memories in a fun and interactive way.

Based in Seattle, we specialize in providing high-quality photo experiences with professional equipment, custom backdrops, and instant sharing capabilities. Every event is unique, and we work closely with you to ensure our service perfectly complements your celebration.

From intimate gatherings to large corporate events, we bring the same level of enthusiasm and professionalism to every occasion. Let's make your event unforgettable!

Our Services

Everything you need for an amazing photo experience at your event

Open Air Photobooth

Our signature open air setup allows for larger groups and more creative shots. No cramped booth - just pure fun and flexibility for your guests.

High-quality instant prints that your guests can take home immediately. Custom templates available to match your event theme.
